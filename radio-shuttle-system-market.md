<p><p>The Radio Shuttle System market comprises two primary types: LIFO (Last In, First Out) and FIFO (First In, First Out). In the LIFO type, the most recently added goods are retrieved first, making it beneficial for managing perishable items or products that have varied turnover rates. Conversely, the FIFO type prioritizes the oldest stock for retrieval, ensuring older items are sold first, which is crucial for maintaining inventory freshness and reducing obsolescence in warehouses. Both types optimize storage efficiency and streamline operations.</p></p>
