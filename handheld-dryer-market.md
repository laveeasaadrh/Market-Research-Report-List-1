<p><p>The handheld dryer market comprises two main types: corded and cordless dryers. Corded dryers are powered by electricity through a plug, offering consistent performance and higher airflow for quick drying. They are typically more powerful and suited for home use. In contrast, cordless dryers operate on rechargeable batteries, providing portability and convenience for travel or on-the-go styling. While they offer flexibility, their drying time may be longer and power less intense compared to corded models, catering to different consumer needs.</p></p>
