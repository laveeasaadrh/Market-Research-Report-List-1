<p><p>The special ceramics market is diverse, encompassing various types based on chemical composition. Oxide ceramics, like alumina, are known for their electrical insulation and thermal stability. Nitride ceramics, such as silicon nitride, offer high strength at elevated temperatures. Carbide ceramics, like silicon carbide, provide excellent hardness and thermal conductivity. Boride ceramics, including titanium diboride, are recognized for their extreme hardness and resistance to wear. Additionally, “Others” encapsulate advanced ceramics with unique properties for specialized applications.</p></p>
