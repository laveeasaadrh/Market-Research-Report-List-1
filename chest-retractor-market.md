<p><p>The chest retractor market consists of two primary types: hand-held hooks and automatic hooks. Automatic hooks, on the other hand, are designed for ease of use, providing consistent retraction with minimal effort. These devices reduce the physical strain on surgeons and enhance surgical efficiency. Both types play crucial roles in improving visibility and access during chest surgeries.</p></p>
