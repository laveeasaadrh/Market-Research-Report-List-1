<p><p>HVACR systems utilize various refrigerants categorized into three main types: fluorocarbons, inorganic, and hydrocarbons/natural fluids. Fluorocarbons, commonly used in air conditioning, are efficient but have environmental concerns due to their greenhouse gas potential. Inorganic refrigerants, such as ammonia and water, are generally more environmentally friendly and efficient but come with safety challenges. Hydrocarbons and natural refrigerants, like propane and isobutane, are gaining popularity due to their low global warming potential and natural sourcing, though flammability is a consideration.</p></p>
