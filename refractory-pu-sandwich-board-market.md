<p><p>The refractory PU sandwich board market comprises three main types: single metal surface, bimetallic surface, and non-metallic surface. Single metal surface boards feature a single layer of metal providing durability and thermal resistance. Bimetallic surface boards combine two different metals, enhancing thermal efficiency and structural integrity. Non-metallic surface boards utilize composites or ceramics, offering lightweight alternatives with excellent insulation properties. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, ensuring optimal thermal management and energy efficiency in various applications.</p></p>
