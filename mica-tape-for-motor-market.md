<p><p>Mica tape is an essential insulation material used in electric motors, offering excellent thermal and electrical properties. Mica glass tape combines natural mica with glass fibers, providing superior thermal stability and mechanical strength, ideal for high-temperature applications. Mica polyester tape, on the other hand, features a polyester backing, making it more flexible and easier to apply, suitable for various insulation needs in lower temperature settings.</p></p>
