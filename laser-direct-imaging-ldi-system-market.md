<p><p>The Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) system market is primarily segmented into two key types: Polygon Mirror at 365nm and Digital Micromirror Device (DMD) at 405nm. The Polygon Mirror 365nm systems utilize a rotating mirror to direct laser beams onto substrates, ensuring high precision in image transfer for PCB manufacturing. On the other hand, the DMD 405nm systems leverage micro-mirrors to modulate light, offering enhanced versatility and resolution for intricate designs, particularly in applications requiring fine details in electronic and photonics industries.</p></p>
