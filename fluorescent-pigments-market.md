<p><ul><li>Thermoset Type</li><li>Thermoplastic Type</li><li>Other (Aqueous Dispersions, etc)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluorescent pigments market is categorized into three primary types: thermoset, thermoplastic, and others, including aqueous dispersions. Thermoset pigments are used in high-temperature applications as they solidify and retain their shape when cured. Thermoplastic pigments, in contrast, can be re-melted and reshaped, offering versatility for various applications. Other types, like aqueous dispersions, provide environmentally friendly options for the formulation of paints and coatings, facilitating better dispersion and application in water-based products. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
