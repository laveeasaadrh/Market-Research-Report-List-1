<p><p>The particulate matter detector market comprises various types based on particle size, notably PM10 and PM2.5. PM10 detectors measure particles with a diameter of 10 micrometers or smaller, which can affect respiratory health. PM2.5 detectors focus on finer particles, 2.5 micrometers or smaller, known for their potential to penetrate deep into the lungs and enter the bloodstream. Additionally, the "Other" category includes detectors for different particle sizes or specific applications, meeting diverse regulatory and environmental monitoring needs.</p></p>
