<p><p>The thermoplastic films market encompasses various types, each with unique properties. Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) offers flexibility and durability, making it ideal for applications like automotive and medical devices. Polycarbonate (PC) is known for its high impact resistance and optical clarity, suitable for safety equipment. PETG/Copolyester balances toughness and clarity, often used in packaging. Acrylic (PMMA) provides excellent transparency and UV resistance, while rigid and flexible PVC are used in construction and consumer products. Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) is valued for its elasticity and adhesion, finding use in footwear and photovoltaic applications.</p></p>
