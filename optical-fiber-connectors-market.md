<p><ul><li>FC Connectors</li><li>SC Connectors</li><li>LC Connectors</li><li>ST Connectors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber connectors market comprises various types, including FC, SC, LC, ST, and others. FC connectors feature a screw-on mechanism, ensuring stable connections, ideal for high-vibration environments. SC connectors utilize a push-pull design for easy coupling and are widely used in telecommunications. LC connectors are compact, making them suitable for high-density applications. ST connectors employ a bayonet-style coupling for quick connect/disconnect and are often used in legacy systems. Other connectors encompass diverse designs for specialized uses in various industries.</p></p>
