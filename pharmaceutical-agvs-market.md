<p><p>Pharmaceutical Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) are categorized into three main types based on their functionality. Unit Load AGVs are designed to transport large, standardized loads efficiently within manufacturing and storage facilities. Automated Forklift AGVs mimic traditional forklifts, handling palletized loads with precision, ideal for tight spaces. Tugger AGVs move multiple carts or trailers in a train-like formation, optimizing material transport over longer distances. Each type enhances logistics, improves safety, and ensures compliance with strict pharmaceutical industry regulations.</p></p>
