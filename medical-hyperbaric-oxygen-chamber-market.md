<p><strong>The Medical Hyperbaric Oxygen Chamber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Pressurized</li><li>Oxygen Pressurized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical hyperbaric oxygen chamber market can be categorized into two main types: air pressurized and oxygen pressurized chambers. Air pressurized chambers use ambient air to create pressure, providing a general environment for various treatments. In contrast, oxygen pressurized chambers utilize pure oxygen for therapeutic purposes, delivering higher concentrations of oxygen at increased pressure. This distinction influences their applications, with oxygen pressurized chambers primarily aimed at specific medical conditions like decompression sickness and carbon monoxide poisoning, while air pressurized chambers are often used for wellness and rehabilitation.</p></p>
