<p><strong>The Thermal Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Reed Switches</li><li>Mercury Switches</li><li>Rod and Tube Thermal Switches</li><li>Gas-Actuated Thermal Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal switches market encompasses various types designed for temperature control applications. Thermal reed switches use magnetic fields to activate at specific temperatures, offering reliable operation. Mercury switches utilize mercury to complete an electrical circuit when heated, providing precise temperature response. Rod and tube thermal switches employ a metal rod within a tube that expands or contracts to trigger the switch mechanism. Gas-actuated thermal switches use gas-filled elements that react to heat changes, ensuring safe and efficient temperature regulation in various systems.</p></p>
