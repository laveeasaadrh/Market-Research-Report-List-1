<p><ul><li>Coriolis</li><li>Magnetic</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Multiphase</li><li>Vortex</li><li>Variable area</li><li>Differential pressure</li><li>Thermal</li><li>Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent flow meter market encompasses various types, each suited for specific applications. Coriolis meters measure mass flow via oscillation, while magnetic meters use electromagnetic fields for conductive fluids. Ultrasonic meters employ sound waves, and multiphase meters handle multiple fluid phases simultaneously. Vortex meters detect flow using vortex shedding, and variable area meters rely on flow area changes. Differential pressure meters gauge pressure drop, thermal meters measure heat transfer, and turbine meters count turbine rotations, each providing unique advantages for accurate flow measurement.</p></p>
