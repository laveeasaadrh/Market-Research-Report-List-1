<p><ul><li>Standard Scanners</li><li>Stereo Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary kiln scanners market is divided into two main types: standard scanners and stereo scanners. Standard scanners utilize laser technology to measure and analyze the kiln's inner surface temperature and alignment, ensuring operational efficiency and safety. In contrast, stereo scanners employ advanced stereoscopic imaging techniques to provide detailed three-dimensional insights into the kiln's condition, enabling precise diagnostics and maintenance planning. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing performance and prolonging the lifespan of rotary kilns in industrial applications.</p></p>
